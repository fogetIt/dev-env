虚拟终端
-----------

:进入: Ctrl+Alt+F1~F6/Right
:复制: Ctrl+Shift+C
:粘贴: Ctrl+Shift+V
:新标签: Ctrl+Shift+T
:关闭标签: Ctrl+Shift+D
:清空: clear
:命令记录: history
:切换 shell:
    .. code-block:: bash

        chsh -s `which bash`
        chsh -s $(which sh)


terminator
----------
- 多窗口终端工具
    - Ctrl+Shift+F
    - Ctrl+Shift+O
    - Ctrl+Shift+E
    - Ctrl+Shift+I
    - Ctrl+D
