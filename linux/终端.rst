虚拟终端
-------
    :进入: Ctrl+Alt+F1~F6/右键
    :复制: Ctrl+Shift+C
    :粘贴: Ctrl+Shift+V
    :新标签: Ctrl+Shift+T
    :关闭标签: Ctrl+Shift+D
    :清空: clear
    :命令记录: history
    :Terminator: 多窗口终端工具（Ctrl+Alt+E/O/I）
    :切换 shell:
        .. code-block:: bash

            chsh -s `which bash`
            chsh -s $(which sh)
